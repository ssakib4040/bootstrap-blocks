Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up your account and start using the platform.

1
Create your workspace

Start by creating a new workspace. Give it a clear name and description that reflects your project. You can always update this later from your settings.

2
Invite your team members

Add colleagues by entering their email addresses. You can assign roles such as Admin, Editor, or Viewer. They will receive an invitation email to join.

3
Connect your data sources

Integrate your existing tools and data sources. We support over 100 integrations including Slack, Google Drive, Notion, and GitHub.

You are all set!

Your workspace is ready. Explore the dashboard, create your first project, and start collaborating with your team right away.
