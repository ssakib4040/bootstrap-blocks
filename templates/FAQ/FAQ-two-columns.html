Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our product and services.

How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up button at the top of the page. Fill in your name, email address, and choose a secure password. You will receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

What is included in the free plan?

The free plan includes access to core features, up to 3 projects, and 1 GB of storage. It is a great way to explore the platform before upgrading.

How secure is my data?

We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for all stored data and TLS for data in transit. Regular backups ensure your data is always safe and recoverable.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your billing settings. Changes take effect immediately and are prorated.

Do you offer team accounts?

Yes, our Team and Enterprise plans support multiple users with role-based permissions. You can invite team members and manage their access from the dashboard.

Where can I find the documentation?

Our full documentation is available at docs.example.com. It covers everything from getting started guides to detailed API references and integration tutorials.
