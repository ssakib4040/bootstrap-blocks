Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

1
What is your refund policy?
We offer a no-questions-asked 30-day refund. If you're not satisfied for any reason, email us within 30 days and we'll process a full refund within 5 business days.
2
How many users can I add to my account?
The Starter plan includes up to 5 seats. The Pro plan includes up to 25, and the Enterprise plan is unlimited. You can add seats à la carte on any plan.
3
Is there a free trial?
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required. After the trial, choose the plan that fits your needs or your account will automatically downgrade to the free tier.
4
Can I export my data?
Absolutely. You can export all your data to CSV or JSON at any time from Settings → Data. Enterprise customers also get direct database export and automatic daily S3 backups.
5
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?
Yes, we offer 50% off for verified non-profits and academic institutions. Contact our sales team with proof of status to get your discount code.
6
What kind of support do you provide?
All plans include community forum access and email support. Pro plans add priority email support with a 24-hour SLA. Enterprise plans include a dedicated success manager and 24/7 phone support.
