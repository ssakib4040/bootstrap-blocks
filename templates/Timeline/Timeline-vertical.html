Our Journey

The story of how we got here, one milestone at a time.

2019
Founded the company

Started in a small garage with three people and a big idea. We set out to solve a problem that millions of people faced every day.

2020
Launched our first product

After 12 months of development and beta testing, we shipped v1.0 to our first 500 customers. The response was overwhelming.

2021
Raised Series A funding

Secured $5M in Series A funding to accelerate growth, expand the team, and invest in infrastructure to support our growing user base.

2022
Reached 10,000 customers

A major milestone — 10,000 businesses across 40 countries were using our platform to streamline their workflows and grow faster.

Today
Expanding globally

With 50,000+ customers and offices on three continents, we are just getting started. The best is yet to come.
